Motors/Motor Control

Nov. 5

I made a little setup to learn some extremely basic motor control, the goal was to prove that I could do position control on a brushed DC motor.

I set up a brushed DC motor using: a Pololu motor driver, an MBED FRDM board, and a Maxon DC motor.

The setup was very easy to set up and control. I am thinking that it would be fun to use a more torque dense brushless DC motor like the Multistar 390kv 16 pole motor.

PID Control

PID controllers are used to control motor speed, position, or torque. They sit on top of other, lower level controllers such as field-oriented control (FOC), which is used to drive brushless motors.
