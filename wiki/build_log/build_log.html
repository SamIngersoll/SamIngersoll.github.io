Motors/Motor Control

Brushed DC motors are much easier to control than Brushless, but are usually much less torque dense.

PID Control

PID controllers are used to control motor speed, position, or torque. They sit on top of other, lower level controllers such as field-oriented control (FOC), which is used to drive brushless motors. A diagram from Tim Wescott's PID Without A PhD representing the PID control loop is below.
